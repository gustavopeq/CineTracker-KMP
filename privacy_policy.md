# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 14, 2026*

CineTracker ("the app") is developed by Gustavo Pequeno. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Name** — used to identify your profile
- **Email address** — used for authentication and account management

If you sign in with Google, your name and email are provided by Google Sign-In.

---

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

- Create and manage your account
- Sync your watchlist, custom lists, and ratings across devices

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

---

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** — used as our backend for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. See [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **The Movie Database (TMDB)** — used to fetch movie and show content. No personal data is shared with TMDB.
- **Google Sign-In** — optional authentication method provided by Google.

---

## Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

- **In the app:** Settings → Delete Account
- **Via request:** [Submit a deletion request](https://forms.gle/Tjk38caUDoq1NBPz9)

All data is permanently deleted upon account deletion with no retention period.
